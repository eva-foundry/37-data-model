# CaaS Business Case: Domain-Driven Consulting Platform

**Executive Summary**: Combine Projects 51-ACA (FinOps), 58-CyberSec, 36-Red-teaming, and 60-IaC into a **$5M-$15M ARR consulting platform** by leveraging the 12-domain ontology.

**Bottom Line**:
- **Year 1 Revenue**: $1.2M (conservative, 20 customers)
- **Year 3 Revenue**: $8.5M (growth path, 150 customers)
- **5-Year Target**: $15M+ ARR
- **Gross Margin**: 60-70% (IaC automation reduces delivery cost)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 14-19x (industry benchmark: 3-5x)

---

## The Opportunity

### What Triggers CaaS Revenue?

Your customers have **3 critical pain points**:

1. **Cost (Domain 10)**: Azure/AWS bills growing 40-50% YoY
   - FinOps program (Project 51) saves 20-30%
   - $200K-$2M annual savings per customer
   - **Customer ROI**: 10-50x on consulting cost

2. **Security (Domains 2+6)**: Compliance failures = business risk
   - Security audit + remediation (Project 58) ensures compliance
   - Breach prevention = $1M-$100M risk avoidance
   - **Customer ROI**: 50-500x on consulting cost

3. **Continuous Testing (Domain 3)**: Static compliance doesn't work
   - Red-teaming agents (Project 36) find exploitable gaps
   - Ongoing remediation ensures security velocity
   - **Customer ROI**: Daily value (prevents incidents)

**Key Insight**: These 3 pain points map to 3 service tiers (Tier 1/2/3). Each domain activation = price lever.
